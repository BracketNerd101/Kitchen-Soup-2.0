Sharon Rychlik, Owner/ Designer

Sharon has NKBA training and 30+ years experience in residential kitchen design primarily in Fairfield, New Haven, and Westchester Counties. She was also a residential builder for fifteen years in Fairfield County and specializes in assisting contractors with their new construction projects. She is skilled in the 20-20 computer design program. She is a member of the National Kitchen & Bath Association, Better Business Bureau, Chaine des Rotisseurs, WIN Networking, AEC-NYC Professionals Circle, and Professional Women of Connecticut.

Greg Heitsman/Designer

Greg attended Miami University and received a Bachelors degree in Environmental Design in 1987. He has been designing kitchens and baths for over 20 years and has his CKD certification by NKBA. He is skilled in the 20-20 computer design program and has been with New Perspective for the past 10 years.

Julia Conforti/Office Manager

Julia is a current student at Norwalk Community College studying Interior Design. She is proficient in AutoCAD, Revit, & Sketchup. Her additional responsibilities include customer service and scheduling deliveries.

Jacqui Williams/Accounting
